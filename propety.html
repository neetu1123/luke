Overview

Nestled in the heart of Bentonville, this stunning Mountain Nest offers the perfect blend of modern comfort and rustic charm. Just minutes from world-class mountain biking trails, Crystal Bridges Museum, and downtown dining, this spacious retreat features three bedrooms, two bathrooms, and can comfortably accommodate up to 6 guests.

The open-concept living area boasts floor-to-ceiling windows with breathtaking views, a stone fireplace, and premium furnishings. The gourmet kitchen comes fully equipped with stainless steel appliances and everything you need to prepare meals at home.

Nestled in the heart of Bentonville, this stunning Mountain Nest offers the perfect blend of modern comfort and rustic charm. Just minutes from world-class mountain biking trails, Crystal Bridges Museum, and downtown dining, this spacious retreat features three bedrooms, two bathrooms, and can comfortably accommodate up to 6 guests.

The open-concept living area boasts floor-to-ceiling windows with breathtaking views, a stone fireplace, and premium furnishings. The gourmet kitchen comes fully equipped with stainless steel appliances and everything you need to prepare meals at home.

Outside, enjoy the expansive deck with outdoor seating, gas grill, and a relaxing hot tub where you can unwind after a day of adventure. The property borders a wooded area, providing privacy and opportunities to spot local wildlife.

All three bedrooms feature premium mattresses and linens, with the primary suite offering a king bed and ensuite bathroom with soaking tub. The second bedroom has a queen bed, while the third bedroom includes two twin beds, perfect for kids or additional guests.

Whether you're visiting for mountain biking adventures, exploring the vibrant art scene, or simply seeking a peaceful retreat in nature, Mountain Nest offers the ideal base for your Bentonville experience.

Short-Term Rental Agreement
This Short-Term Rental Agreement (“Agreement”) is made effective as of the date of a confirmed reservation through Airbnb, Guesty, VRBO, or other booking platforms (collectively, “Booking Platform”), by and between the registered guest (“Tenant”) and Bentonville Lodging Co LLC (“Landlord”).

1. The Parties
This Agreement is entered into between the individual or entity named on the confirmed reservation (“Tenant”) and Bentonville Lodging Co LLC (“Landlord”).
2. The Property
The “Property” refers to the specific home or unit associated with the confirmed reservation.
3. Termination
    • Landlord may inspect the Property with prior notice as required by applicable Arkansas law.
    • If Tenant violates this Agreement or the house rules provided in the listing, Landlord may terminate the stay immediately without refund.
    • Tenant waives all rights to process if they fail to vacate upon termination or at the scheduled checkout time and date.
4. Maintenance & Repairs
Tenant shall maintain the Property in good, clean, and rentable condition, using it only in a lawful manner. The Property must be left in a condition immediately habitable by the next guest, requiring only routine cleaning. Damages or excessive cleaning will result in deductions from Tenant’s security deposit.
5. Trash
All waste shall be disposed of in designated receptacles. No trash or debris may be left inside or outside the Property.
6. Pets
    • Only dogs are permitted at select properties.
    • Guests must disclose dogs in advance and pay the applicable pet fee.
    • Failure to disclose a pet will result in forfeiture of the security deposit.
7. Quiet Enjoyment
Tenant shall behave in a respectful manner toward neighbors and not create disturbances.
    • Quiet hours: 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM (no outdoor noise).
    • Guests not included in the reservation are not allowed on the Property during quiet hours.
Violation may result in immediate termination of the stay.
8. Smoking
Smoking of any kind is prohibited inside the Property. Any violation will result in forfeiture of the full security deposit.
9. Liability
Tenant and their guests agree to indemnify and hold harmless Landlord from any claims of injury, loss, or damage arising from use of the Property. Landlord’s insurance does not cover Tenant’s personal property; Tenant is encouraged to secure their own insurance.
10. Security Deposit
The security deposit is refundable if no damage occurs. Deductions may be applied for damages, missing items, or violations of this Agreement.
11. Attorney’s Fees
Tenant shall be responsible for reasonable attorney’s fees and costs incurred by Landlord in enforcing this Agreement.
12. Use of Property
This Agreement is for transient occupancy only. Tenant does not gain residency or tenancy rights.
13. Shortened Stays & Conditions
No refunds are issued for shortened stays, weather conditions, acts of God, or events beyond Landlord’s control.
14. Firearms
Only legally owned and permitted firearms are allowed in accordance with state and local laws. Firearms must remain properly stored and may not be discharged on the Property.
15. Fireworks
Fireworks or hazardous materials are strictly prohibited. Violation is grounds for immediate eviction.
16. Illegal Use
The Property may be used only for lawful purposes. Any illegal activity—including but not limited to drug use, underage drinking, or harboring fugitives—will result in immediate eviction without refund.
17. Possessions
Valuables left behind will be held for up to 90 days. After that period, unclaimed items become the property of the Landlord.
18. Television & Streaming
TV and streaming devices are provided. No paid purchases may be made. Any unauthorized charges will be billed to Tenant.
19. Internet
High-speed wireless internet is provided as a convenience, but no refunds will be issued for outages, speed issues, or service interruptions.
20. Cancellation Policy
Each Property may have its own cancellation policy posted on the Booking Platform. Tenants must review the cancellation policy prior to booking.
    • Generally: Reservations booked more than 72 hours in advance may be canceled within 48 hours for a full refund.
    • After that, the posted cancellation policy applies.
    • Weather, personal changes of plan, or other non-Landlord factors do not qualify for refunds.
    • Extenuating circumstances may apply as defined by the Booking Platform.
21. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed under the laws of the State of Arkansas.

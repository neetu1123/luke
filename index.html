July 26, 2025
We had a great stay at The Gables house in Callicoon. The house was clean and organized, some parts of the house were even nicer than the photos, the beds were comfy, and the house clean and spacious. The kitchen may be the most well-stocked we have seen in terms of lots of pots/pans, knives that actually cut, large bowls, dishes, gadgets, cookies utensils, etc. Luke was very responsive and informative.
